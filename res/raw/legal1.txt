1. Grab a friend to be a rebounder and ONE basketball to shoot

2. A 3 second count down will give you time to get ready. When you hear the beep START shooting. 

3. Make the most free throw shots you can in 1 minute. REMEMBER TO KEEP SCORE. When you hear another beep STOP shooting. 

4. Click upload to upload your current video or hit Record Again to try again.

5. Type in the number of shots you made in the "score" box 

6. To submit your score make sure you register with your email
