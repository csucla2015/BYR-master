1. Grab a friend to record, a bunch of quarters, and a standard 1.5 ounce shot glass.

2. Place the shot glass at any length away from the edge of the table. 

3. A 5 second count down will give you time to get in position. 

4. When you hear the beep, bounce the most cups you can into the shot glass in 10 seconds. 

5. Type the number of shots you made in the "score" box or use the arrows to increase or decrease your score. 

6. Click Upload Now to record your current video or hit Record Again to try again.
